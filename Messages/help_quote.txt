There are 8 leagues available:

    - SERIE A
    - SERIE B
    - PREMIER LEAGUE
    - PRIMERA DIVISION
    - BUNDESLIGA
    - LIGUE 1
    - EREDIVISIE
    - CHAMPIONS LEAGUE

Bets available:

    - ESITO FINALE 1X2
    - UNDER/OVER
    - ESITO 1 TEMPO 1X2
    - UNDER/OVER PRIMO TEMPO
    - UNDER/OVER SECONDO TEMPO
    - GOAL/NO GOAL
    - ESITO FINALE 1X2 HANDICAP
    - ESITO FINALE 1X2 + GOAL/NOGOAL
    - ESITO FINALE 1X2 + U/O
    - DOPPIA CHANCE + UNDER/OVER
    - GOAL/NOGOAL + U/O 2,5
    - ESITO 1 TEMPO/FINALE
    - 1X2 PT + 1X2 ST
    - DOPPIA CHANCE + GOAL/NO GOAL

Use the command /match to receive all the matches scheduled for that day. Ex:

    - /match ven
    - /match sab
    - /match dom

Available days are: lun, mar, mer, gio, ven, sab, dom.
Once you have chosen the match, use the command /get team to receive all the quotes relative to the match played by team. Ex:

    - /get milan
    - /get barca
    - /get psg

To place your bet use again the command /get.
In this case the message has to be sent in the form: /get team_bet.

    Example1: /get milan_x pt
    Example2: /get milan_gg + over 2.5
    Example3: /get milan_under 3.5 pt

Command MUST be separated from the bet.

    - correct: /get chievo_1h
    - wrong: /getchievo_1h

Blank spaces in the bet do NOT matter. Separators ',' and '.' are equivalent.

In case the bet is relative to CHAMPIONS LEAGUE insert an '*' before the team name.

    - correct: *juve_x pt

IMPORTANT:

    1. 'team' can be either team1 or team2, it does NOT matter.
    2. 'team' and 'bet' ALWAYS need to be separated by '_'.
    3. CAPS do NOT matter: 'milan' and 'MILAN' they both work.

Each team has one or more alias. For example, for DEPORTIVO LA CORUNA you can use one of the following:

    - DEPORTIVO LA CORUNA
    - DEPORTIVO
    - DEPOR

To check the complete list of all teams use the command /alias .
More commands:

/confirm: confirm the bet

/cancel: delete a bet which is 'Not Confirmed'

/delete: delete a bet which is 'Confirmed'

/play: place the bet. It requires the amount of euros as input.
Ex: /play 3

/summary: list of bets already chosen by other users
