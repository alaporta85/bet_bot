/help_quote: Instructions to place the bet correctly.

/getquote: Look for the bet and send a message showing all
           the details. You will be asked to either confirm
           or cancel the bet.

/confirm: Confirm the bet.

/cancel: Cancel the bet.

/play: Go to Lottomatica, login and place the bet. It requires the amount of
       euros as input. Ex: /play 3

/update: Update the database after the results are updated in Lottomatica.

/summary: List of bets already chosen by other users.

/score: Graph showing the % of success of each user.

/aver_quote: Average quote for each user.

/best: Highest WINNING quote

/worst: Lowest LOSING quote
